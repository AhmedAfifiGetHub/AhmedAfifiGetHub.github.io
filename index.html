01 · Featured Project

E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard

Analysed 500K+ transaction records from the Olist Brazilian e-commerce dataset. Cleaned and aggregated data with SQL and Python, then built an interactive Tableau dashboard enabling drill-down by region, product category, and time period. Focused on revenue trends, delivery performance, and customer review scores.

Key Finding
Delivery delays exceeding 3 days correlated with a 34% drop in review scores. On-time deliveries averaged 4.2★ vs 1.9★ for delays over 7 days — suggesting a hard SLA threshold logistics teams should target.
